Project Info

I am a huge fan of Scoot Networks, a company that facilitates efficient and reliable transportation via shared, electric, smartphone-activated motorscooters. I have taken hundreds of scoot rides and I have had a blast zipping through San Francisco in zero-emission electric mopeds. Although I love the service, I have been disappointed with their mobile product because it falls short in terms of UX design. Along with the UX short-comings, I felt that with transportation platform is missing out on their incredible potential for utilizing ride data to enrich the user experience. Taking this into consideration, I recreated the application and added several features that make it easier for users to carry out essential tasks such as looking up parking info, viewing location data, and booking a reservation. After building a prototype, I sent it over to their team for further review.
