Project Info

In February 2015, I was hired as a contractor to assist the Everlane design team with their web and mobile products. The team is working on improving the current web experience and introducing new features that will commonly used in the new mobile app. I use modern prototyping tools like Sketch and Framer to effectively communicate designed interactions to all stakeholders (designers, management, engineers, etc.) in each project. For web based projects, I build templates from scratch and add interactivity with javascript and animation libraries such as GSAP and Velocity.js.

Scoot Prototype

External Links

  • Scoot Prototype

Project Info

I am a huge fan of Scoot Networks, a company that facilitates efficient and reliable transportation via shared, electric, smartphone-activated motorscooters. I have taken hundreds of scoot rides and I have had a blast zipping through San Francisco in zero-emission electric mopeds. Although I love the service, I have been disappointed with their mobile product because it falls short in terms of UX design. Along with the UX short-comings, I felt that with transportation platform is missing out on their incredible potential for utilizing ride data to enrich the user experience. Taking this into consideration, I recreated the application and added several features that make it easier for users to carry out essential tasks such as looking up parking info, viewing location data, and booking a reservation. After building a prototype, I sent it over to their team for further review. The prototype was warming accepted by the people at Scoot Networks and we’re currently discussing possibilities for new projects that can be incorporated into the Scoot mobile product.

Project Info

Revolver Yoga is an esteemed local business in my hometown, Walla Walla, WA. Owner and instructor, Courtney Morgan, asked me to design an elegant website that would ultimately increase online bookings through thoughtfully tailored UX design. Here’s what Courtney has to say about the site:

“What an absolute pleasure it has been to work with Matt and see the amazing website for my business come together quickly and without any stress (on my end, at least). It has been so easy to collaborate with him, to tell him my needs, ideas, vision, and how my clients need the site to function to get them into the door. Matt's ability to integrate not only a super functional blog interface but also, and most importantly, our external booking site for classes and workshops has made the transition from curiosity to client commitment so much simpler. His clean, edited, and stylish design aesthetic has proven time and again to impress potential clients and bring them into the studio.

Working with Matt has been a match from day one, and I couldn't be happier. I highly recommend him to any of my friends, clients, or business connections who are looking for a designer who genuinely cares about his work; from the initial steps of creating the concept, communication, and ideal personalized functionality to a clear aesthetic that represents their branding. Matt will be my designer for as long as he will take on my projects!”
