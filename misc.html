Project Info

This is a collection of projects that I pursued outside of my regular work. During my second year at University of San Francisco, my advisor, Scoot Murray, peaked my interest in design and code. Since then, I’ve been tinkering with all sorts of platforms for creating screen based art and design. Processing and Paper.js are by far my favorite tools, but I’ve also had a great time learning how to use prototyping tools such as Framer. Enjoy!
